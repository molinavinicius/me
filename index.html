I am a computer engineer from USP (University of São Paulo), with emphasis in data science, currently living in São Carlos/SP, Brazil. With a strong math background, through ICMC (Institute of Mathematical and Computational Sciences at USP), I have a passion for delving into the concepts behind each subject. Addicted to asking the whys and always questioning the status quo. I have great appreciation for subjects like paradigms, design principles, software architecture, philosophy, literature, statistics, data analysis and product development.

I had used some tech in production systems such as Python/Django, NodeJS, TypeScript, Postgres, Redis, AWS such as Lambda, RDS, EC2, DynamoDB, and experience in contact centers focusing on A2P messaging and phone calls for user experience and engagement.
